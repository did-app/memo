Roadmap

Features Mission Pricing Contact

We are investing heavily in Memo's future and continuing to help users reclaim focus through high quality communication.

This roadmap lets you know what features are coming next. If you would like to contribute by suggesting feature ideas or giving feedback we would love to hear from you.

Currently in development

  • Conversation threads with unique topics.

  • Assigning highlighted questions to specific contacts.

  • Live attachments view (show all per contact or conversation).

  • Thread conclusions (with optional voting).

  • Inbox delay / scheduled delivery.

  • Shared pins.

  • Bold, italic and titles.

  • Add media helper in command bar.

  • Search command bar on home page.

Next in line

  • Receive email in Memo.

  • Contact screener to pick and choose what you let into your inbox.

  • Calendaring / appointment booking integration.

  • Rich map link previews.

  • Action first inbox view.

  • Shared pins.

  • Expectation setting.

  • Advanced conversation search with keyword highlighting.
